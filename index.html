What is MAP2?

Hello! We are MAP2, and we're a tour map solution company. We help you to create easily customizable tour maps and manage them without hassle.

How MAP2 works

  • Points of Interest - Destinations

    Start the map creation by adding the destinations. Search the name of city, then MAP2 system will find and place a marker on the correct location and name of the city. You can mark different types of destinations - staying in hotel or maybe just a visit.

  • Connect the Dots - Transportations

    How the travelers transport from one city to another? Use various types of lines to differenciate each transportation.

  • Make It Yours - Customizing

    Change colors of the map, shapes of markers, style of transportation lines - Anything you need to change to make the map yours. Try our goodlooking style presets!

  • The Map is Ready! - Export

    The map is ready to use! You can export the map as interactive map to include on your website, or export the map as JPG for simple display of map on a website or to include in a printing copy.

  • Real-time Map Updates

    When there is only a couple of changes needed to be made on the map, it can be a pain in the square to find and replace the old map. When you export the map's URL(instead of downloading as image), the map will be automatically updated when you make changes on a map.
